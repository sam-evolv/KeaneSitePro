Objective
The top-left header/logo rises out of the black toolbar at mid-widths (tablet/half-screen). Fix ONLY the header logo sizing and alignment for that breakpoint. Do not change desktop (>=1201px) or mobile (<=767px) layouts and do not touch nav styles, spacing, or markup beyond adding a stable class if missing.

Steps
1) Identify the site header element that contains the logo and nav (e.g., <header>, .site-header, .navbar). If it lacks a unique class, add class="site-header" to that element in the HTML.
