Overview
Our professional delapodation service specialises in the safe removal and strip-out of non-structural elements from buildings and sites. We prepare properties for renovation, refurbishment, or redevelopment with minimal disruption, ensuring all work is carried out efficiently and responsibly.

What's Included
- Interior and exterior strip-out works
- Removal of fixtures, fittings, and partitions
- Site clearance of non-structural materials
- Careful handling of reusable or recyclable elements
- Safe disposal of debris and unwanted materials
- Full preparation for the next stage of development

Delapodation Scope
Building Interiors
- Partitions and stud walls
- Ceilings and flooring
- Fixtures and fittings
- Insulation and non-structural materials

External Works
- Sheds, garages, and small outbuildings
- Fences and boundary features
- Garden structures
- Hard landscaping elements

Our Process
1
Site Assessment
Inspection of structures and identification of non-structural elements to be removed

2
Planning & Coordination
Scheduling, logistics, and safety planning tailored to your project needs

3
Strip-Out & Clearance
Systematic removal of designated elements with minimal disruption

4
Handover & Preparation
Site cleared, cleaned, and made ready for renovation or redevelopment

Compliance & Safety
All delapodation works are carried out in accordance with health and safety regulations. We prioritise responsible handling, recycling wherever possible, and ensure your site is left safe, compliant, and ready for its next stage.
